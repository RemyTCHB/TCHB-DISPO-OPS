PHASE I: INTEGRITY & INTAKE Verification
1
Step 1

Underwrite (UW) the Deal

AI Underwriting Engine

Run complete subject property metrics and comp profiles directly through the **AI UW Tool** to evaluate and confirm wholesale purchase margins. Double check calculations to prevent faulty deploy rates.

Underwritten stats must be locked in to establish verified parameters for Step 6 (InvestorLift Assets).
2
Step 2

Search Entire CRM Database

DND Safety Scrubbing

Search the seller's active mobile and landline numbers across our unified CRM. Immediately verify and configure the seller profile to either **Disqualified (DQ'd)** or **Do Not Disturb (DND)**.

Critical Seller Guardrail Protocol

This compliance step is mandatory to ensure our home seller is never targeted with automated buyer marketing blasts or text notifications referring directly to their own house.

PHASE II: HYPER-LOCAL BUYER SOURCING
3
Step 3

Pull Local Hyper-Qualified Buyers

Local Data Mining

Target and extract information on local cash buyers active inside the property's sub-market. Construct a buyer spreadsheet matching precise localized parameters:

Target Pricing Delta

Query cash properties sold within +/- $10,000 to $15,000 of our target asking rate.

Budget Alignment
Corporate Intelligence

Pull purchasing LLC details, then extract their direct Point of Contact (POC) using OpenCorporates.

Owner Tracing
Agent Mobile Digging

Isolate and log direct contact info of representing agents for those transactions.

Mobile Numbers Only
Zillow Extraction Note: Many interfaces hide real estate agents' phone numbers behind central brokerage desk lines. Click into biography listings or query local agent registers to obtain their true personal mobile number.
4
Step 4

Join Local Facebook Groups

Social Planting

Request access to localized Real Estate Investing (REI), wholesale, and county-specific cash buyer groups on Facebook. Doing this in advance ensures you are approved and ready to post when the syndication drafts are signed off.

5
Step 5

Immediately Begin Investor Scraping

Investorbase Mining

Extract active regional buyer cohorts directly using our specialized **Investorbase** scraping credentials. Filter searches to target active cash buyers in the property's sub-market.

1-Hour Proximity Rule

Historical Lead Mapping

If our newly acquired property is located within **1 hour** of any previously marketed or closed deals, instantly query those past transactions to assemble highly relevant Smart Lists for our SMS outreach database.

PHASE IV: SYNDICATION & BROADCAST outreach
7
Step 7

Prepare Realtor Outreach Database

Outreach Logistics

Build an active list of localized buyer's agents operating within the property's sub-market. Execute this extraction using our dual-track logistics workflow:

7.1
Individual Search

Manually search agent directories on Zillow, Realtor.com, and local MLS resources representing transactions near our subject asset.

7.2
Macro Scraping Extraction

Deploy local scraping tools to automatically extract details of active registered agents operating within the targets ZIP codes.

7.3
Format List & Import

Clean, organize, and format the harvested lists (First Name, Last Name, Mobile, Email, Brokerage) and import them directly into our CRM under target tags.

8
Step 8

Create Investor Text Blast Draft

SMS Broadcast

Draft the investor SMS campaign using your compiled Hyper-Qualified Smart Lists. Optimize the text to feature: **Property Address/Submarket, Price, ARV, Rehab Estimate, and direct link to photos**. Keep on standby in Draft status, and dispatch immediately once approved.

Step 9

SMS Broadcast

Tailor an SMS sequence focused on buyer's agents. Incentivize outreach by clearly highlighting broker-to-broker commission splits, flexible showing schedules, and direct access terms. Keep in Draft status, and dispatch immediately once authorized.
